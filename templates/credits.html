This project aims to revolutionize the way people access and interact with public transportation information in the Washington Metropolitan Area. By leveraging the capabilities of the WMATA API, we aim to provide a seamless and intuitive interface for users to plan their trips and stay updated on the latest transportation news and disruptions.

Our team is dedicated to delivering the most up-to-date and accurate information to users in real-time, so they can make informed decisions about their travel plans. In addition to providing basic trip planning functionality, we also aim to incorporate advanced features such as personalized route recommendations and real-time tracking of buses and trains.

We believe that our project has the potential to significantly improve the commuting experience for residents and visitors of the Washington Metropolitan Area. Whether you're a seasoned commuter or a tourist exploring the city, we hope that our platform will become a go-to resource for all of your public transportation needs.
